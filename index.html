About me

Hi, my name is Florian Tanner. I am a PhD student and freelance data analysis and visualization expert. I can help you with:

  • Advice on how to tell your story with your data
  • Visualization of your data as charts, graphs and maps
  • Discovering trends and patterns in your data with statistics
  • Presentation of complex data to support decision-making

For inquiries you can contact me via this website and follow my work on Twitter and Github.
